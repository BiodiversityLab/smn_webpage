About the network

We support researchers, infrastructure staff, and stakeholders using metabarcoding and eDNA to answer ecological questions and inform biodiversity management.

Why we exist

Metabarcoding workflows are powerful, but choices in sampling, lab protocols, and bioinformatics can strongly affect results. We help align practices and improve reproducibility.

What we do

We host knowledge exchange (seminars, working groups, docs), recommend reporting standards, and connect projects across Sweden.

Where data goes

We promote FAIR outputs and integration with national infrastructure‚Äîespecially the molecular services and tools within SBDI.
