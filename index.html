About the network

The Swedish Metabarcoding Network supports coordination, knowledge sharing, and harmonized approaches for metabarcoding across environments and taxa. The network is funded by SciLifeLab.

Shared best practices

Guidelines for sampling, contamination control, marker choice, reference databases, and reporting.

Interoperable data

Promote FAIR workflows and publishing via national infrastructure like SBDI and the ASV portal.

Training & community

Workshops, office hours, and peer support across lab, bioinformatics, and data management.
